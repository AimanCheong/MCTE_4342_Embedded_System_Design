# Push Button Counter

8 LEDs are connected to Arduino Uno and a program is written to blink (On and Off) based on the time given below.

LED | On Time (ms) | Off time (ms)
------------ | -------------|--------------
1 | 781 | 515
2 | 2014 | 1348
3 | 343 | 573 
4 | 678 | 1839
5 | 342 | 534
6 | 1478 | 326
7 | 1859 | 351
8 | 777 | 888
